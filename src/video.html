Video Systems

THE MAGIC OF THE MOVING IMAGE

From commercials on enormous screens in Times Square to iPhones showing YouTube clips, the public is getting accustomed to seeing high definition moving images in both large and small formats. Momentum sees that incorporating the moving image into events is not just the way of the future but is here now.

To that end, Momentum has invested in high-powered projectors, LED video walls plus media servers and video switches to suit any event. In addition, we have plasma/LED/LCD displays of all sizes for all types of applications from corporate to retail.

Momentum loves to work with clients on pushing the envelope with their event videos mapping skills. From 3-D video mapping on a 20-foot high 3-D mountain at a product launch to video synchronized with a moving light show at a dance party to LED/LCD screens and vintage TVs imaginatively arranged for an art installation, the Oxygen event video experience is a spectacular assault on the senses.
