EVENT LIGHTING

Setting the Right Mood

Lighting is transformative by nature. In the right person’s hands, lighting for events can convert a drab lifeless area into a bright oasis of color and liveliness. Lighting is not only about illuminating a room or reflecting a desired mood but about creating the energy and pulse of an event much as music can do. This is the Oxygen Event Lighting Philosophy.

See how we transformed these corporate and entertainment events.

We use GLP, Martin Professional, and Chauvet Professional gear. GLP is a pioneer in LED intelligent light systems, while Martin Professional fixtures have dominated the industry for 20 years. Chauvet Professional lights are recognized for unmatched quality and value.

Our team will work with you to create the perfect lighting support structures, whether they are hung from architecture or supported on the ground. Safety and elegance are top priorities. Oxygen also offers custom gobos, providing a seamless way to highlight your brand.

Sampling of Techniques We Employ

For Corporate Events:

  • Wireless LED uplighting
  • Feature product lighting
  • Wireless table pinspotting
  • Vivid tones on walls
  • Textural relief lighting
  • Custom gobos
  • Light podiums and catwalks

For Entertainment Events:

  • Moving lights
  • Effect lighting (Strobes and blinders)
  • Giant discoballs
  • Lasers
  • Atmospheric effects (haze and low-lying fog)
